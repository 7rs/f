# F  

  A font for developers.  

## Goals  

- Support CJK Characters
- Support Ligatures
- Support Nerd Fonts
- Support Emoji

## Policy  

  Use simplest and fastest tools.  

<!--  -->
- Rye
- uv
- Ruff
<!--  -->

  And recommend the use of the following.  

<!--  -->
- VSCode or VSCodium
- Alacritty
- Nushell or Fish
- Starship
<!--  -->

## Get Started

  rye sync
